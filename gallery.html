Photography Styles

Portrait Photography

Portrait photography focuses on capturing the personality and mood of an individual or group. It often involves close-ups and can be taken in a studio or natural settings.

Landscape Photography

Landscape photography captures the beauty of nature and the outdoors. It often includes wide-angle shots of mountains, forests, and other natural scenes.

Event Photography

Event photography documents special occasions such as weddings, parties, and corporate events. It aims to capture the emotions and moments of the event.
